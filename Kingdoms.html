Kingdoms/Countries/Sovereignties

What to Consider

Think about the major political entities in your world. Each kingdom, country, or sovereign state should have:

  • A unique name and identity
  • Geographical boundaries and capitals
  • Government structure and ruling system
  • Military strength and defenses
  • Economic resources and trade relationships
  • Cultural values and traditions
  • Relationships with other nations (allies, enemies, neutral)

Getting Started

Begin by listing the major powers in your world. Ask yourself:

  • How many kingdoms/countries exist?
  • Which are the most powerful?
  • What conflicts exist between them?
  • Are there any alliances or treaties?
  • How has history shaped current relationships?
